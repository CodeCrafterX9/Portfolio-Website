About Me

Hey, I'm Prachi.

I'm an Analyst at Physics Wallah, based in Noida I work with SQL, Python, and BI tools to build reports, dashboards, matviews, and automated data workflows that support business operations across multiple verticals.

I graduated from IGDTUW in 2025 with a BTech in Information Technology. At PW, my work involves writing complex SQL queries, setting up Airflow DAGs for automated reporting and payout calculation, building Power BI dashboards, and helping migrate data queries between warehouses like Redshift and Trino.

I'm genuinely interested in how data systems work end-to-end — not just the analysis part, but how data is collected, cleaned, stored, and served. I spend my free time exploring new tools, building small projects, and learning from the community.
